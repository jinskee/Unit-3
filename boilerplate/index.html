Amount of Publicland Owned by Government Agency in the Western United States

This interactive looks at publicland per state, represented by a choropleth map and coordinated visual barchart.

The publicland represented is either owned by one of four federal agencies or the state.

As you can see, the amount of acres owned by a respective agency in each state varies from a small amount to very large amount, making the barchart exclude smaller values. Hover over a state with the mouse cursor to retrieve the acres owned by the government agency you select.

Data from National Resource Council of Maine Publicland breakdown
